About us

A home where you will find that healing touch that will cure all your health worries is here. Medimall is that one place where you will find anything to everything. With us, the perfect doctors, best medicines, and all your health essentials are just a click away. It doesn't matter if you are from an urban city or a rural village, consult your doctor from the comfort of your home, and we will deliver you fast cures at your doorstep.

Medimall, founded in 2020, by the expert team of Mr.Fahad KM, Mr.Fayis KM, and Dr.Sufeekath, with the goal of providing affordable, accessible, and authentic Healthcare services to both rural and urban populations.
